# Terms Draft

This is a draft for closed MVP testing and must be reviewed before public release.

## Service Purpose

Diet Coach helps users create and adjust diet plans when real life changes the original plan.

## User Responsibility

Users choose whether to approve and follow generated plans or revisions.

## Not Medical Advice

The service is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency support. Users should consult a qualified professional for medical concerns.

## AI Output

AI-generated plans and revisions may be inaccurate or incomplete. The app requires user review before plan changes are persisted.

## MVP Testing

During the closed MVP, features may change and data may be used to improve product quality and reliability.
