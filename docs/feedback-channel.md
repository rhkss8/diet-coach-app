# Feedback Channel

The closed MVP needs one clear feedback destination before tester recruitment.

## Recommended Setup

Use `EXPO_PUBLIC_FEEDBACK_URL` for the app-facing feedback link.

Good options:

- `mailto:support@example.com`
- a private Typeform or Google Form URL
- a Discord, Slack, or Kakao channel invite URL for testers

## Feedback To Collect

- where the plan felt hard to continue
- whether adjustment felt calming or annoying
- whether the revised plan was specific enough
- whether the user returned after a broken day
- bugs, unclear copy, and data concerns

## MVP Rule

Keep one feedback channel for the first tester cohort so responses do not split across tools.
